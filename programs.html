Talent Development Initiatives

We offer various programs to help students identify and nurture their talents

KNP Talent Day

Our flagship event held every semester where students showcase their talents in various categories including music, dance, comedy, poetry, art, and technical innovations.

  • Held twice per academic year
  • Awards for top performers
  • Open to all KNP students
