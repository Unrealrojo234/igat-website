Talent Development Initiatives

We offer various programs to help students identify and nurture their talents

Flagship Program Twice per year

KNP Talent Day

Our flagship event held every semester where students showcase their talents in various categories including music, dance, comedy, poetry, art, and technical innovations.

  • Open to all KNP students
  • Awards for top performers
  • Judged by industry professionals
  • Networking opportunities
Register Now More details
Event Structure:
  • Morning: Preliminary auditions
  • Afternoon: Main showcase
  • Evening: Awards ceremony
Categories:
  • Performing Arts (Music, Dance, Drama)
  • Visual Arts (Painting, Drawing, Photography)
  • Technical Innovations
  • Literary Arts (Poetry, Spoken Word)
