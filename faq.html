Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about IGAT Club

Membership Questions

All Kisii National Polytechnic students are welcome to join IGAT Club. You can register during our recruitment drives at the beginning of each semester or visit our office in the Student Affairs building. There are no membership fees.

No, IGAT Club membership is completely free for all Kisii National Polytechnic students. Our activities are funded through sponsorships and donations.

Absolutely! Many students join IGAT Club precisely to discover their hidden talents. Our talent identification workshops and activities are designed to help you uncover abilities you may not have recognized in yourself.

Program Questions

We support a wide range of talents including but not limited to:

  • Music (vocal and instrumental)
  • Dance and performing arts
  • Visual arts (painting, drawing, photography)
  • Creative writing and poetry
  • Technical innovations and inventions
  • Sports and physical talents
  • Public speaking and debate

If you have a talent that's not listed, we'd still love to hear about it!

KNP Talent Day is held twice per academic year - once each semester. The exact dates are announced at the beginning of each semester.

Yes, students are welcome to participate in multiple talent categories, though we recommend focusing on your strongest areas to allow time for proper preparation.

Support & Partnership Questions

We welcome partnerships with organizations that share our vision for talent development. Partnership opportunities include:

  • Sponsoring events or programs
  • Providing internship opportunities for our talented students
  • Offering mentorship or workshop facilitation
  • Collaborating on talent development research
  • Funding scholarships for talented students

Please contact us to discuss potential partnership opportunities.

Donations support various club activities including:

  • Organizing talent shows and competitions
  • Purchasing equipment and materials for workshops
  • Bringing in professional trainers and mentors
  • Funding student participation in external events
  • Providing awards and incentives for outstanding talents
  • Covering administrative and operational costs

We provide regular reports to donors on how their contributions are being used.

Yes! We welcome volunteers in various capacities:

  • Workshop facilitators in specific talent areas
  • Event organizers and coordinators
  • Competition judges
  • Mentors for talented students
  • Fundraising and outreach support

Please visit our Support Us page or contact us directly to discuss volunteer opportunities.

Alumni Questions

Absolutely! We value our alumni and offer several ways to stay involved:

  • Return as mentors or workshop facilitators
  • Participate as judges in competitions
  • Share your success stories to inspire current students
  • Support through donations or sponsorships
  • Participate in alumni networking events

Many of our most successful alumni maintain strong connections with the club.

Alumni can contribute in many ways:

  • Financial donations to support club activities
  • Sharing professional expertise through workshops
  • Providing internship or employment opportunities
  • Mentoring current students
  • Helping to expand our professional network
  • Sharing your IGAT success story to inspire others

Contact us to discuss how you'd like to contribute.

Still Have Questions?

If you didn't find the answer to your question, feel free to contact us directly.
