Our Story

Our Story

IGAT (I Got A Talent) Club was founded in 2018 at Kisii National Polytechnic by Mrs. Peris Bosibori Ongori with the vision of creating a platform where students could discover, develop, and showcase their talents beyond academic excellence.

Recognizing that society often focuses solely on academic achievement while overlooking the immense potential in various talents, IGAT Club was established to bridge this gap. Winston Churchill's observation that "the graves carry billionaires of all time because society never took time to identify talents among them" served as a key inspiration for the club's formation.

Today, the club has grown to become a vibrant community that identifies and nurtures talents across all departments of Kisii National Polytechnic, regardless of year of study or gender.

Our Mission

To identify, nurture, and develop talents among students at Kisii National Polytechnic, creating future stars and innovators who will transform society through their unique abilities.

Our Vision

To become the leading talent identification and development platform in technical institutions, producing globally competitive individuals who excel in both their professions and talents.
